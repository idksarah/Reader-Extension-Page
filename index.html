Thank you for installing!

Reader-Extension is a Chrome Extension designed to assist ESL learners, primarily those whose native language is Spanish. Highlighting a word will trigger a popup with that words translation into Spanish, and after pressing okay, will trigger a speech to text of the word.

It was designed within 12 hours at a hackathon, so it's a little janky,, and kind of ugly... but I will attempt to continue to update to maintain and add more features. Have fun!
